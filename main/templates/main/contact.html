Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How do I book a meet and greet session?

Booking is simple! Browse our celebrity listings, select the celebrity you'd like to meet, choose an available date and time, and complete the booking form. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What happens if I need to cancel my booking?

We understand plans change. You can cancel your booking up to 48 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to our cancellation policy.

How long do meet and greet sessions last?

Most sessions last between 15-30 minutes, but this can vary depending on the celebrity and the package you select. The exact duration will be specified on the booking page.

Can I request a specific celebrity not listed on your site?

Yes! We're always expanding our network. If you don't see your favorite celebrity listed, contact us with your request and we'll do our best to arrange a meeting.
