Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the membership valid?

Memberships are valid for one year from the date of approval.

How do I receive my membership card?

Once your application is approved, we'll send you a digital membership card via email. Physical cards are mailed to the address provided during registration.

Can I upgrade my membership tier?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time by paying the difference between your current tier and the new tier.

How do I use my membership benefits?

Your membership ID will be automatically applied when you log in to make bookings. For in-person events, simply present your membership card.
